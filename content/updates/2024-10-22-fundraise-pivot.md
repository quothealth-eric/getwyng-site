### Option A: Raise Pre-Seed Now

- Dilution: 15-25%
- Timeline: 3-6 months of full-time fundraising

### Option B: Raise F&F, Launch, Then Pre-Seed
- F&F valuation: Less formal (SAFE, no cap or high cap)
- Amount: $250K-$1M
- Dilution: 5-10%
- Timeline: 4-8 weeks
- Then raise pre-seed with **traction** at 2x+ better terms

I chose Option B.

1. **Preserve equity early.** Every % matters when you're solo.

3. **Speed to market.** I should be building, not pitching. F&F is faster.

4. **Raise from strength.** When I go back to VCs, I want to negotiate, not beg.
